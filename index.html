About Me

My name is Emmy!

Hi there! I am a passionate web developer with a strong foundation in front-end technologies, UX/UI design, and database management. Currently in my final year at Wake Tech Community College, I’m earning my associate degree in web development, along with multiple certifications in WordPress, React, JavaScript, and accessibility. I enjoy crafting user-friendly, responsive websites and have experience working with tools like Figma, Photoshop, and Canva to bring creative ideas to life. Beyond coding, I love hiking, reading fantasy and sci-fi books, baking, and playing video games—especially JRPGs. I’m always excited to learn new technologies and take on projects that challenge me to grow as a developer. Feel free to check out my work and connect with me!
